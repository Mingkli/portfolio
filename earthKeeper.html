Earthkeeper

Concept

This is an interactive device designed to enhance social interaction and engagement by assigning participants a character based on the personality they wish to adopt during the event.

This device also assigns different storylines to each character, encouraging interaction with other users. Participants also can choose whom they want to interact with, and the device will generate new storylines based on these interactions. This device transforms social gatherings into immersive, role-playing experiences, making it easier for attendees to connect and enjoy the event.
